Early life

Alan Mathison Turing was born on June 23, 1912, in Maida Vale, London, England. At the age of 24, he proved that there cannot exist any universal algorithmic method of determining truth in mathematics. This paper not only introduced the “Turing Machine”, but is also widely acknowledged as the foundation of research in artificial Intelligence.
